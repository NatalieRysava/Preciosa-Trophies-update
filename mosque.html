    Crystal Mosque of Uzbekistan

    The many various types of production technology that we have at our disposal mean that Preciosa is able to create crystal replicas of specific buildings, unique architectural structures, or figures. The work is undertaken based on supplied photographs and ideally also drawings. We prepare the resulting crystal work down to the very last detail. We combine bold crystal pieces with our smallest Preciosa cut stones.

    We also offer minimalist designs, in which we let the unique nature of the colors or technologies such as glass molding, cutting, or sandblasting stand out. The glassmakers at the Preciosa workshop have created copies of the Imam al-Bukhari Mausoleum, a mihrab, or the Kaaba.
