    Crystal Logo

    GQ, the internationally renowned gentlemen’s lifestyle magazine, announced its Man of the Year in Lisbon, Portugal. This prestigious title is awarded to the most influential and successful men in the world. Upon this occasion, 20 crystal trophies, which were decorated with 6000 cut stones, were produced. The glass team at Preciosa stuck them on stone by stone for a total of 600 hours.
